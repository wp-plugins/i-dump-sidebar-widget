The i-Dump Social Widget will show down your uploaded photos made with your iPhone in the sidebar.

== Description ==

The i-Dump Social Widget will show down your uploaded photos made with your iPhone in the sidebar. Of course you can setup your desired settings for the Widget. For this plugin you need the WP-Dump application, available in the AppStore on the iPhone and the 'i-Dump' plugin.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `i-dump-social-widget` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to 'Appearance' -> 'Widgets' into your WordPress Menu, and see the new 'i-Dump Social Widget' widget.
4. Setup your desired settings, so how many pictures you want to show down into your sidebar and what will be the 'width' and 'height' for all pictures
